*Authored by Aaron Price, 2026-07-01. This is the final, authoritative word on product naming — supersedes any naming language in VISION.md or MASTER_BUILD_PROMPT.md. If those documents ever conflict with this one, this one wins.*

*Amended 2026-07-09 (Aaron): the AI's public name is **EILA** — that is the trademark, always spelled EILA. The app-wide rebrand shipped the same day. Code internals (file names like `lib/ila.ts`, API paths) intentionally keep `ila`.*

---

## Company

**Commissioned 41 LLC** — the legal company and parent brand.

## Public Product Name

**EILA**

This is what customers see, remember, talk about, subscribe to, and build a relationship with.

Public language should be:

> Meet EILA.
> Your AI performance companion for commission-based work.

**Not:**

> Download MissionOS Lite.

## Underlying Product / Platform Name

**MissionOS Lite**

MissionOS Lite is the operating system layer behind EILA — the internal product architecture, intelligence system, data layer, performance engine, dashboard framework, commission tracker, follow-up engine, and daily mission system that powers the public EILA experience.

In simple terms:

> EILA is the product users fall in love with.
> MissionOS Lite is the system that powers her.

## Relationship Between EILA and MissionOS Lite

**EILA is the public product. MissionOS Lite is the operating system behind the product.**

That means:

- The app icon can say **EILA**
- The website can say **Meet EILA**
- The onboarding can say **Welcome to EILA**
- The assistant introduces herself as **EILA**
- The subscription can say **EILA Pro** or **EILA by Commissioned 41**
- Technical docs can say **Powered by MissionOS Lite**
- Internal architecture can still reference **MissionOS Lite**
- Dealer MissionOS can eventually integrate with the broader MissionOS ecosystem

This gives the best of both worlds: a human product name people remember, and a scalable operating system architecture behind it.
