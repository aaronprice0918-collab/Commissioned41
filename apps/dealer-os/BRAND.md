# Commissioned 41 — Brand & Mission Narrative

**This is the canonical brand story and voice. Source of truth for all copy,
marketing, and "About" surfaces. Keep wording consistent with this.**

Tagline (Commissioned 41 — the company): **Know Your Mission. Execute With Purpose.**
Tagline (MissionOS — the dealership product): **Live Data. Real Accountability.**

---

## What the name means (the heart of the brand)

**Commissioned** — from the **Great Commission**: Christ commissioned us to go and
tell the world the good news of Jesus Christ, our Lord and Savior (Matthew 28).

**41** — a **new beginning, the chapter after the season of testing.** Throughout
Scripture, 40 is the trial; the 41st is the breakthrough:
- The flood fell for 40 days and 40 nights — on the **41st**, the sun came up.
- Israel wandered the desert for 40 years — in the **41st**, they reached the Promised Land.
- Jesus was in the wilderness for 40 days — on the **41st**, He came out.

**Commissioned 41 stands for a new beginning, a new chapter** — for the founder
personally, and for everyone the company is built to serve. Hold this meaning
with sincerity; it's faith-rooted, never preachy or used as a gimmick.

---

## The hierarchy (read this first — it's the whole model)

**Commissioned 41 is the parent company — and it's where the big mission lives.**
It has many "tentacles" (products) reaching into different parts of life and
business over time.

**MissionOS is ONE tentacle: the Dealership Operating System.** It is the first
product Commissioned 41 has shipped, and it's tied to the car dealership world.
MissionOS is not the whole vision — it's the first expression of it.

```
Commissioned 41  ← the company / the mission / the tentacles
   └─ MissionOS   ← tentacle #1: the Dealership Operating System (live flagship)
   └─ (future tentacles: more tools for life & business)
```

Do NOT attribute the broad life mission to "MissionOS." The broad mission is
**Commissioned 41's.** MissionOS = the dealership product.

---

## The Manifesto (Commissioned 41)

Commissioned 41 was born from a simple realization: most people drift through
life without a clear mission, operating reactively instead of intentionally.
Commissioned 41 was created to change that.

Commissioned 41 exists to help people take control of their lives, align their
actions with their purpose, and execute relentlessly toward their goals.

It began as one man's personal operating system — a framework to fulfill the
mission God placed on his life: creating financial freedom for his family,
becoming the husband and father he is called to be, growing spiritually, and
building a legacy that outlives him.

But it was never meant to stop with him. What started as a personal system is
becoming a company that builds tools to empower others to do the same — in
business and in life.

At Commissioned 41, we believe every person has been commissioned for a purpose.
We build the tools that help them accomplish it. **MissionOS is the first.**

## The product: MissionOS (the Dealership Operating System)

MissionOS runs the whole dealership floor on one system — leads, desking, F&I,
live leaderboards everyone trusts, and an AI sales manager (Jimmy) that coaches
every rep. Built by a finance director with 20 years in the car business.

- **MissionOS positioning line:** "The Dealership Operating System."
- **MissionOS tagline:** "Live Data. Real Accountability."
- Note: the current C41 logo art still shows "Live Data. Real Accountability." under the wordmark — that line now belongs to MissionOS; update the logo lockup when convenient (or keep the logo tagline-free).
- **Right now, the public face leads with MissionOS / the dealership** — earn
  dealers' trust by nailing this product first; the broader Commissioned 41
  vision (more tentacles) comes later.
- **Jimmy** is MissionOS's in-store coach (tenant-facing). **ILA** is Commissioned
  41's company-level AI — she sits above the products and steers the business.
